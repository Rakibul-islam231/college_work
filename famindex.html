Mymensingh

famous for padry/rice

Rice is our main food. It is also the main item of food for many other people of the world. We take rice regularly as the staple food of our eating. Rice is collected from paddy. But Bangladesh is not capable of growing as much rice as we need.
The marshy land and hot and moist climate of the Tropical countries are suitable for rice. So, it grows greatly in Japan, China, Myanmar, India, Thailand, the Philippines, and Bangladesh. Rice is also grown in Italy, Russia, Egypt, and some parts of America.

Chapainawabganj famous for mengo

Mengo is one kind of froute. It is made from the leaves of the mengo plant. Now-a-days, mengo is the most popular drink all over the world. We can not do a single day without it. Tea grows in plenty in China, Japan, Indonesia, India, Bangladesh and Sri Lanka. Tea plant is grown in hilly places where there is much rainfall as well as sunshine. But, the rain water must not stay at the root at the plant. Tea seeds are sown in a nursery in the rainy season. Then they are planted in an especially prepared ground. The preparation of tea is very easy. Tea leaves are boiled in water. Sugar and milk are also mixed with it. Thus, we get a tasteful cup of tea. The necessity of tea beggars description. A cup of tea gives us energy. It refreshes our mind and body. Tea also contributes to our national economy. Bangladesh earns a lot of foreign currency by exporting tea. Therefore, we should take care of its increased production.
